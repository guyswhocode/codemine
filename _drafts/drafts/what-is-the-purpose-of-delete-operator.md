---
title: What is the purpose of delete operator 
categories: ["programming"] 
tags: ["JS"]
---
    The delete keyword is used to delete the property as well as its value.
    ```javascript
    var user= {name: "John", age:20};
    delete user.age;

    console.log(user); // {name: "John"}
    ```
